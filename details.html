Instructions

  1. Cook the rice and let it cool.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan.
  3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add diced carrot and peas. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  5. Push vegetables to one side of the pan, and crack eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs.
  6. Add the cooled rice to the pan and mix well with the vegetables and eggs.
  7. Drizzle soy sauce over the rice and stir to combine.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally, until the fried rice is heated through.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
